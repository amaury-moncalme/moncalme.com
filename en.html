Amaury — Avant-Garde Artist & Wellness Practitioner

Music and touch form a single flow, a kind of universal language. Every sound massage tells a singular story, from Imsouane in Morocco to French heritage sites and Dubai.

A DJ recognized on the Parisian and Moroccan scenes, Amaury has spent over 8 years exploring the sonic territories of ambient, traditional and electronic music. It's this deep immersion in the power of sound that naturally led him to sound massage.

From Imsouane in Morocco to French heritage sites and Dubai, every composition is born from an encounter between place, moment and emotion.
